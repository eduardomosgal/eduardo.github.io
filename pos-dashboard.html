Project Case Study

POS Analytics Dashboard

Turning complex restaurant data into clear, actionable insights

Overview

A fully implemented analytics dashboard module for a POS system, designed to help restaurant owners understand performance through data visualization.

The Problem

Key information like top-selling products or peak hours was hidden in raw data, making decision-making inconsistent and reactive.

The Process

My approach focused on reducing complexity and prioritizing key insights over raw information.

Hover to see Wireframe

Outcome

The final dashboard allows users to track sales, identify top products, and detect activity peak hours through intuitive heatmaps. This solution transforms raw data into a visual narrative that simplifies complex business management.

Reflection

Designing for data-heavy environments taught me that less is truly more. The challenge wasn't just displaying numbers, but providing actionable insights at a glance. I learned how to balance visual hierarchy with density, ensuring that business owners can make critical decisions in seconds without feeling overwhelmed.
